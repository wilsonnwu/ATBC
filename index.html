WELCOME

Enter into his gates with Thanksgiving and into his courts with praise - Psalms 100:4

Come and worship our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ as we sing praises with traditional and  contemporary India Telugu songs and music by the choir. But most of all, come and witness and hear the inspiring messages from our Pastor Rev David Vidyasagar.

OUR MISSION

Antioch Telugu Baptist Church is dedicated to minister the Word of God to the Indian community with the increased sensitivity to the needs of the Telugu Christians in Chicago and suburban areas. Also, to provide Christian fellowship to the cross section of Indians, to foster closer ties with Christians around greater Chicago and suburbs and to introduce to our children and youth the Christian values and tradition of worship.
